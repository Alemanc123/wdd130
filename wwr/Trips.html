Our Trips

Peru Rafting

Experience the thrill of rafting through the rivers of Peru.

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the heart of Peru's pristine wilderness. Navigate the rapids of the Amazon basin, surrounded by lush rainforests and breathtaking landscapes. Whether you are a seasoned rafter or a beginner, our expert guides will ensure a safe and unforgettable adventure. Discover the beauty and excitement of Peru's rivers and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Salmon River Experience

Join us for an unforgettable adventure on the Salmon River.

Dive into the excitement of the Salmon River, known as the "River of No Return." This trip offers a perfect blend of thrilling rapids and serene stretches, ideal for both adventure seekers and nature lovers. Marvel at the stunning canyons, wildlife, and clear waters as you paddle through one of the most scenic river journeys in North America. With top-notch guides and all the necessary gear provided, you can focus on enjoying the ride and the spectacular views.

Nile River Explorers

Explore the mighty Nile River with our expert guides.

Venture into the heart of Africa with our Nile River Explorers trip. Experience the thrill of rafting on the world's longest river, navigating through a mix of intense rapids and calm waters. This adventure takes you through diverse landscapes, from dense jungles to open savannas, offering a unique perspective on the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region. Our experienced guides will lead you safely through the challenges, making this an epic journey you will never forget.
